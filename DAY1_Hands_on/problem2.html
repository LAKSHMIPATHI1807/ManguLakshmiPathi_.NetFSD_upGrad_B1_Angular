About

Welcome to Lucky Restaurant, a perfect place where delicious food meets a pleasant ambience. Located in the heart of the city, our restaurant offers a wide variety of freshly prepared dishes including traditional, Chinese, and fast food items at affordable prices. With a clean and hygienic environment, comfortable seating, and friendly staff, we ensure a delightful dining experience for families, friends, and students. Our special signature dishes and exciting opening offers make every visit memorable. We also provide takeaway and home delivery services to serve you better. Visit Spice Garden to enjoy quality food, great taste, and warm hospitality.
